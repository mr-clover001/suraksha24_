Choosing someone to care for an aging parent or recovering loved one is one of the hardest decisions a family makes. It's not just about medical competence — it's about trust, communication, and peace of mind. At Suraksha24, we've worked with hundreds of Kolkata families going through this exact decision, and we've seen what separates a good choice from a stressful one.

## 1. Check for verified, licensed nursing staff

Always ask whether nurses are GNM/BSc-qualified and registered with the Nursing Council. A trustworthy provider will share credentials without hesitation.

## 2. Understand what "skilled nursing" actually covers

Skilled nursing isn't just companionship — it includes wound care, catheter and Ryle's tube management, injections, vitals monitoring, medication administration, and post-surgical care. Ask for a written scope of service.

## 3. Ask about supervision, not just staffing

A single nurse working in isolation is riskier than one backed by a clinical supervisor who reviews cases regularly. At Suraksha24, every nursing case is monitored by a care coordinator who checks in with the family and the nurse.

## 4. Background verification is non-negotiable

Since caregivers enter your home, insist on police verification, ID proof, and reference checks. Don't be shy about asking for documentation.

<Callout type="tip" title="Ask this on the first call">
  "Can you show me the nurse's registration and background verification before the first visit?" A confident, specific answer — not a vague reassurance — tells you a great deal about the provider.
</Callout>

## 5. Flexibility of shifts

Needs change — a 24-hour case may reduce to 12 hours as recovery progresses. Choose a provider that can scale care up or down without long lock-in contracts.

## 6. Communication with family

Especially important for NRI families or those living in a different city, ask how updates are shared — daily reports, photos, or calls.

## 7. Emergency backup

What happens if the assigned nurse falls sick? Ask how quickly a replacement is arranged. At Suraksha24, we guarantee backup staffing within hours, not days.
