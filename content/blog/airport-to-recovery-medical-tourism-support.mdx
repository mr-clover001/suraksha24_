Traveling to another country for medical treatment is stressful enough without also having to manage logistics alone — arranging transport, finding accommodation, navigating an unfamiliar hospital system, all while dealing with illness. At Suraksha24, we built our International Patient Services specifically to remove that burden. Here's what the full journey looks like with our support.

## Before you travel: planning and preparation

Our process begins before you even board a flight. We collect your medical records and reports for review by the appropriate Kolkata specialist, arrange a remote consultation to confirm the treatment plan where possible, provide a written cost estimate covering consultation, procedure, and stay, and assist with medical visa documentation and hospital invitation letters.

## Arrival: airport to accommodation

On arrival in Kolkata, our team receives you directly at the airport, arranges transport to your pre-booked accommodation, and ensures everything — from local SIM cards to basic orientation — is handled before your first hospital visit.

## During treatment: coordination that actually reduces stress

Throughout your treatment, we provide a dedicated care coordinator to manage hospital appointments and communication, accompany patients where language or comfort support is needed, arrange local transport between accommodation, hospital, and diagnostic centers, and keep family members back home updated on progress.

## After discharge: recovery support

This is where many medical tourism providers stop — but recovery often matters as much as the procedure itself. We arrange skilled nursing care at your accommodation if needed post-surgery, coordinate follow-up consultations and any additional diagnostics, and provide physiotherapy support for surgical or orthopedic recovery before you travel home.

<Callout type="tip" title="Before departure: a safe return">
  Before you head back to Bangladesh, we ensure discharge summaries and reports are properly organized for your local doctor, confirm you're medically fit for travel, and remain available for follow-up coordination even after you've returned home.
</Callout>

## Why this matters

A medical journey isn't just the procedure — it's everything around it. Families dealing with a health crisis shouldn't also have to become logistics experts in a foreign city. That's the problem we set out to solve.

## The Suraksha24 difference

Because we're fundamentally a home healthcare company, our international patient support doesn't end at hospital discharge — we're equipped to extend nursing, physiotherapy, and recovery care right through to the point you're ready to travel home.
